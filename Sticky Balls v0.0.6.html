How to Play

Objective

Clear all bubbles from the grid before any bubble reaches the red danger line. Pop groups of 3 or more matching colours to score points and level up.

Controls

  • • Drag / Swipe — aim the shooter
  • • Release / Tap — fire

Scoring

  • • Pop 3–4 bubbles  — 10 pts each (×1.0)
  • • Pop 5–7 bubbles  — 10 pts each (×1.3 combo)
  • • Pop 8+ bubbles   — 10 pts each (×1.6 chain)
  • • Orphaned bubbles — 8 pts each (×level)
  • All point values scale with your current level.

Mechanics

  • • Orphans — bubbles left floating after a pop fall away for a bonus
  • • Miss streak — 3 shots that don't pop anything add a new row
  • • Pressure — new rows push down automatically, faster each level
  • • Ball speed — shots get faster as your score grows
  • • Efficiency — final score is multiplied by your pops-per-shot ratio

Level Up

Clear every bubble to advance. Each level adds more starting rows. Ball speed increases continuously as your score grows.
